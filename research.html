Sensor Architecture Design

Sensors are in integral part of a system, providing knowledge about system states, through state estimation, which can further be utilized to control the system. The problem of sensor design addresses questions regarding, \(\color{red}{{}^1}\)which type of sensors do we need, how accurate sensors do we need, and when and where do we use them.
