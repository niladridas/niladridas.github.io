About me

I received my Bachelors and Masters degree in Electrical engineering from Jadavpur University (JU) and Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur (IIT) in India, in 2012 and 2014, respectively. I worked as a project associate for a year at IIT Kanpur. In September 2015, I joined the PhD program in Aerospace engineering at Texas A&M University, under the supervision of Raktim Bhattacharya.

I served as the student mentor, for the Aerospace Engineering Graduate Student Council at TAMU (academic Year 2018). I was also an Aerospace Engineering Graduate Student Council member (academic Year 2017), and the Graduate and Professional Student Council delegate of Aerospace Department of TAMU. (academic Year 2017)
